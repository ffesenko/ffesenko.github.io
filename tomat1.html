Agridera Fresh Market Carrot Varieties

*****

Agridera aims to be one of the world's leading companies in the breeding, production, and marketing of carrot seeds. We invest intensively in research and development to provide solutions to growers and consumers in numerous countries around the world. In particular, we breed improved disease-resistant cultivars adapted to different markets globally.

Currently, emphasis is placed on developing varieties that offer resistance to pests, diseases, and environmental stress, as well as improving flavor, color, and root shape. Moreover, comprehensive efforts are being undertaken to breed carrots with enhanced storage and shelf life qualities.

In terms of horticultural traits, breeding focuses on earliness, different root sizes, firmness, color, flavor, crack resistance, shelf life, and high/low temperature adaptability. Combinations of quality features and disease resistance are sought in both short and long-root varieties.

Agridera Carrot breeding programs are focusing on three main segments: Early Varieties, Storage Varieties, and High-Yield Varieties.

Early Carrot Breeding Program

This breeding is aimed for the European and North American markets. Focus is on early maturing varieties that are perfect for fresh market consumption and salads. These varieties have high resistance to common diseases and pests, and produce tender, sweet roots.

Storage Carrots

This breeding is for markets in colder regions such as Northern Europe and Canada. The main focus is on varieties that store well and maintain quality over long periods. These carrots have improved resistance to cracking and disease, and are perfect for winter storage.

High-Yield Carrots

This breeding is for markets requiring high-volume production, such as the United States and China. We focus on high-yielding carrot varieties with excellent disease resistance, suitable for both fresh market sales and processing. These carrots have a consistent, uniform shape and size, making them ideal for large-scale farming.

Agridera operates through international and local distributors around the world.
