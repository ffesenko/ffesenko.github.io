Tomato Determinate Red Standard Round

*****

Very high yield and excellent fruit quality!

Characteristics

Resistance: HR: ToMV:0-2; Fol: 1,2; Ve/Vd; IR: TYLCV

Plant

  • Strong vigor providing good leaf coverage.
  • Very high productivity with good fruit setting.
  • Early-matured variety.

Fruit

  • Long shelf life on plant and post-harvest.
  • Nice shiny deep red color.
  • Average fruit size: 140-160 grams.

Cycle

  • Spring
  • Fall
  • Winter

Color

Red
